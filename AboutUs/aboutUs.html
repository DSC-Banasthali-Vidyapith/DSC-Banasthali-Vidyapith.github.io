ABOUT US

We think slightly out of the box, we believe that a club's resources must not only be channeled into conducting events but also to propagate learning and teaching, symbiotically.




AIM

Taking the first step is the most difficult feat to achieve so let's begin and explore together. Developer Student Clubs is here to help you learn and grow by solving and developing innovative solutions. We aim to build an amazing Development Ecosystem where we bring Developers, Designers and Managers under one roof to inspire thousands of budding developers like you.

VISION

Our vision is to empower students and developers to learn about a wide range of technologies and apply their new learnings to create great solutions for real world problems. It is our endeavour to be the right mentor for you. Learning is the most important part of DSC, it helps an individual to apply right set of skills and help others to grow ahead in their career.

MISSION

In DSC, students grow their knowledge in a peer-to-peer learning environment and build solutions for local businesses and their community. We help in solving your queries as you develop your skills and help you grow your kowledge by building innovative solutions. Our mission is to encourage and mentor aspiring developers.

Community Guidelines



BE NICE
We're all part of the same community, so be friendly, be welcoming, and generally be a nice person. Be someone that other people want to be around. We want the platform to be an excellent experience for everyone regardless of gender identity and expression. Treat everyone with respect. Participate while acknowledging that everyone deserves to be here.
BE RESPECTFUL AND CONSTRUCTIVE
We're a very diverse group (100+ countries and counting!). Assume positive intent. The person making you feel uncomfortable may not be aware of what they are doing, and politely bringing their behavior to their attention is encouraged. Remember to be respectful and constructive with your communication to your fellow organizers, harassment is not tolerated. Everyone should take responsibility for the community and take the initiative to diffuse tension as early as possible.
BE COLLABORATIVE
Work together! We can learn a lot from each other. Share knowledge, integrate efforts and help each other out.
PARTICIPATE
This community is for YOU, so we encourage you to be a part of it! Join us in our Upcoming events, offer feedback, and follow us on our social media platforms for updates.
